I created analysis sheets to assist me ace my university examinations, and they were quite helpful.

These sheets are a compilation of all the questions that have been posed in the past 4-5 years of university examinations (MUHS) and in my college terminals and prelims
These sheets are colour coded, and the best way to use them is to stick to the codes.

  • Red questions: are the most frequently asked and significant / so do these first
  • Blue questions: are less frequently repeated than the red ones, but contain the majority of important topics (50-60%)
  • Black questions: are the least frequently asked and are often miscellaneous / do these after the red and blue ones
  • Green questions: if present are for an extra boost (not asked in unis till date)

So, how did the idea of building a website come to me?

The prime motive was to make my tools (such as Analysis sheets) available to students like me.
I noticed that there isn't much organised information in my course, so I organised it. This helped me ace my academics pretty well, and I wanted to pass on the good work

Later, it evolved into what it is now (version-1):
a place where you can get not just academic resources, but also other cool stuff like my notion template, phone wallpapers, my medium link, and other things I've done in the past. (I'll make sure to keep optimising it so that it can be more useful.)
